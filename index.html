Frequently Asked Questions

Common Mobile Service Queries

Got questions about operational rates, warranty coverage, or camp approvals? We have prepared quick summaries below.

Do you work directly with RV warranty providers or insurance?

Yes. We provide comprehensive written diagnostics, clear photos of the damage, and detailed line-item estimates required by major extended warranty providers (such as RV Complete, CornerStone, XtraRide) and insurance companies. We will help you compile the exact paperwork needed to get your reimbursement claim processed smoothly.

Are mobile technicians allowed to perform repairs inside campgrounds?

Absolutely. Private, provincial, and national campgrounds welcome Gabriel's Mobile RV Repair because we are fully licensed, register at the main office upon arrival, and carry robust liability insurance. We maintain a clean, professional workspace and strictly observe all campground safety guidelines.

What happens if you don't have the required replacement part on your service truck?

While we stock a broad supply of universal mechanical components, switches, capacitors, and water fittings, custom items (like bespoke slide tracks or specific awning models) may need to be ordered. If that happens, we will secure your rig for travel, order parts through our wholesale dealer network, and dispatch back to install them the moment they arrive.

What are your baseline emergency travel and labor fees?

We charge a flat dispatch fee (ranging from $35.00 within Moncton up to $120.00 for long-range priority calls) to cover the fuel and travel expenses of driving our shop to your location. Standard labor is billed hourly at highly competitive rates, and we always outline costs before starting any physical repairs.
